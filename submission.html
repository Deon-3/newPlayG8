Please note that all music submissions are subject to approval, as we are regulated by RiSA (Recording Industry of South Africa) and must adhere to limitations on the number of music works included on our jukeboxes.

Music Submission Requirements:

1. Audio Quality: Music files must be at least 128 kbps and of high audio quality.
2. Track Information: All submissions must include clear and accurate details, such as track names, album titles, featured artists, and any other relevant information.
3. Album Artwork: Album images must be clear, sharp, and at least 350 x 350 pixels in resolution.
4. Genre Classification: Submissions must specify the genre category of the music works.

We appreciate your understanding and adherence to these requirements.
