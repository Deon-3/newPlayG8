Playgate, originally established as Coinplay by Christie Lötter in the early 2000s, has built a reputation for delivering high-quality, innovative software solutions. Since the launch of its first jukebox in 2005, the company has consistently set industry standards for excellence.

In 2017, the company rebranded as Playgate to better reflect its expanded vision and to align with its relocation to the Western Cape, South Africa. This strategic shift allowed Playgate to venture into non-commercial markets where the Coinplay name was less suitable.

At Playgate, we specialize in designing, developing, manufacturing, and supplying premium digital audio and video solutions for both public and private venues. Our product range includes advanced digital touchscreen jukeboxes, cutting-edge music solutions, video advertising systems, and cloud-based media services, all crafted to meet the highest standards of quality and innovation.

Our Mission
We provide state-of-the-art digital jukebox products and services designed to empower commercial coin operators and venue owners. Our solutions help maximize profits, streamline costs, and offer complete control over music and advertising campaigns, delivering value and efficiency at every level.

Franchise Dynamics

Franchise Governance: Playgate Franchises operate under group policies that are established, enforced, and regulated collectively by the franchise members themselves.
Annual International Conference: These policies are reviewed and formalized annually at our international conference, ensuring a unified code of conduct across the group.
Decentralized Decision-Making: The management of the Playgate Franchise group is determined collaboratively by the franchises, not solely by the Head Office.
Fostering Collaboration and Protection: This collaborative approach safeguards the interests of all franchise members, fostering unparalleled cooperation and strong bonds of friendship within the Playgate community.

Our Business Plan

When the concept of commercial coin operators collaborating toward a shared goal was first introduced, many in the industry were skeptical, claiming, "It will never work." At the time, competition was fierce, with operators viewed as rivals battling over territory. There was no code of conduct to regulate the industry and no established channels for resolving disputes.

Today, thanks to the measures we’ve implemented since Playgate’s inception, we have become one of the strongest and most cohesive groups in the coin-op industry, demonstrating the power of collaboration and shared values.
