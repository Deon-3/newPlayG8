Advertising Methods

1. Squeezed-Back Video or Static Advertisements

  • These advertisements wrap around the left and bottom sections of the video display area, offering a unique way to capture attention.
    1. Video Ads: Moving visuals that grab the audience's focus and effectively convey your message.
    2. Static Ads: Display a portion of an image, ideal for quick-turnaround campaigns.
    3. Specifications:
      1. Resolution: 1280 x 720 pixels (720p)
      2. Image Format: JPG
      3. Display Area: 331,700 square pixels (256 x 720 pixels for the left section, 1280 x 145 pixels for the bottom section)
      4. Maximum Duration: 15 seconds
      5. Display Frequency: One-minute intervals during music video playback (without sound).
    2. Cross-Fade Video Advertisements
  • Cross-fade ads are displayed during the transition between tracks, utilizing the jukebox's seamless cross-fade feature to maintain flow while advertising.
    • Specifications:
      1. Resolution: 1280 x 720 pixels (720p)
      2. Display Area: 921,600 square pixels
      3. Maximum Duration: 10 seconds (without sound).
    3. Television-Style Video Advertisements
  • These advertisements resemble traditional TV commercials and include sound. They are played during empty queue times with one-minute intervals.
    • Specifications:
      1. Resolution: 1280 x 720 pixels (720p)
      2. Display Area: 921,600 square pixels
      3. Duration: 10 seconds to 2 minutes.
    4. Static Advertisements
  • Static advertisements are displayed as images on the video screen during audio track playback or when the queue is empty.
    • Specifications:
      1. Resolution: 1280 x 720 pixels (720p)
      2. Image Format: JPG
      3. Display Area: Up to 921,600 square pixels
      4. Rotation: Every 15 seconds (adjustable).

    2. Resonate with the audience by delivering a meaningful and relatable message.
    3. Communicate how the product or service enhances the consumer's life.
    4. Represent values that go beyond the product itself.
    5. Be inextricably linked to the brand, ensuring clear association.
