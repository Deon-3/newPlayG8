At Playgate, we prioritize exclusivity and strong partnerships, which is why we do not sell or distribute our jukeboxes directly to individuals,
pubs, taverns, or bars. Instead, we supply our products exclusively through our appointed network of dealers, franchises, and partners.
This approach ensures a trusted relationship with our franchisees and protects their investments, providing them with the confidence
and peace of mind needed to grow their businesses.

Franchise Qualification Criteria

To qualify as a Playgate Digital Jukebox Franchise, applicants must meet the following criteria (not applicable for dealer or distributor applications
outside Africa):

1. Industry Experience

  1. Proven history as a well-established commercial coin operator with at least 20 jukeboxes in operation.
  2. Relevant experience in the coin-op industry.

2. Business Background:

  1. If no coin-op experience, a strong business track record or relevant experience will be considered.

3. Music Rights:

  1. Must secure approved Music Rights Usage licenses for the intended country of operation.

4. Training and Engagement:

  1. Commitment to regular training on our software.
  2. Willingness to attend the annual Playgate group conference periodically to stay informed about industry developments and product launches.

5. Operational Commitment:

  1. Purchase or conversion of no fewer than 20 jukeboxes.
  2. Adherence to the code of conduct established by the existing network of franchises in the country.

6. Cultural Fit:

  1. Ability to integrate effectively within the group dynamics.

7. Franchise Area Principal Approval:

  1. In certain provinces or regions, franchise applications must be approved by a Franchise Area Principal.
